How to Play

Click a tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it. Arrange tiles in order with the empty space last.

Use New to randomize a solvable board, and Reset to restore the board you started the session with. Try the solver with Solve For Me or step through the solution with Step.

Choose a mode from the Start screen: Normal, Timer (countdown), or Limited Moves.
